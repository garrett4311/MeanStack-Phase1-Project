Jungle Champion Descriptions

Wawick

Warwick is an aggressive early jungler who excels in 1-on-1 fights. He is great at controlling objectives and powerfarming once he obtains tiamat.

Kha'Zix

Kha'Zix is an assassin whose primary goal is to isolate the enemy carries. He is easy to learn but hard to master. He can be played as a pure assassin or as a bruiser, each playstyle having their own benefits.

Elise

Elise is one the few AP viable junglers. Her primary strength is her ability to gank and consistently towerdive opponents during the early game. She's great at setting up and securing kills for her team.
